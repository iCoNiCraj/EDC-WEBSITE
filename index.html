ABOUT US

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell, NIT Durgapur is the institution’s official Entrepreneurship Promoting student body. Through a plethora of initiatives throughout the year, we remain steadfast to our sole dream of creating a sustainable and diverse entrepreneurial ecosystem where the young and the elderly come together to make entrepreneurship a common reality in India.

Innovation and Incubation Council

Innovation and Incubation Council, NIT Durgapur is NIT Durgapur’s very own incubation body, established under the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India. EDC, NIT Durgapur is an integral part of IIIC, NIT Durgapur and works together with the faculty members of IIIC to carry out various entrepreneurial activities and projects in the campus

Our Events

EDC’s numerous events enjoys overwhelming participation from students in NIT Durgapur, and across the country. Some of our flagship events are: E-SUMMIT:The second largest E-Summit in eastern India, graced by the biggest entrepreneurs BizzCup: A fun event designed exclusively for the freshmen students. Pitchfest: A nationwide B-Plan Competition, with huge funding and mentorship opportunities for winning proposals.

Our Journey

In 2015, inspired by a desire to instil an entrepreneurial culture in NIT Durgapur, a group of three friends decided to start the first independent Entrepreneurship Cell of the College. What began as a small endeavour by three young minds has today risen to stand tall with over 70 members, achieving unimaginable things in just a span of few years.
